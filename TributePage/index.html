A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

Here's the timeline of Abdul Kalam's Life:

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, TamilNadu
  • 1960 - Graduates in Aeronautical Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology
  • 1980s-1990s : As the chief of the Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme, he was responsible for the development and operationalisation of AGNI and PRITHVI Missiles.
  • 1992-1999 : works as Chief Scientific Advisor to the PM and Secretary of the DRDO.
  • 2002-2007 : President of India
  • 2015 - Death
